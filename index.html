Privacy Policy

GenreSense is designed to recognize music genres while keeping audio processing on your device.

Information we collect

GenreSense does not require an account and does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs. We do not collect or upload your microphone recordings, recognition history, or genre results to our servers.

Microphone and audio

Microphone access is used only when you start genre recognition. Audio is analyzed on your device. Recognition clips and history may be saved locally so you can revisit them. They are not uploaded by GenreSense.

Local data

App preferences, recognition history, explored genres, and saved audio clips are stored locally. You can remove history within the app or delete the app to remove its locally stored data.

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. GenreSense does not receive or store your payment-card details. Apple's handling of purchase information is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

External services and links

GenreSense may open services such as Apple Music, Spotify, Wikipedia, or Wikimedia when you choose a related link. Those services operate under their own privacy policies. Recognition itself does not require an internet connection.

Children's privacy

GenreSense does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The latest version will be published here with its effective date.
